About

I'm a Senior Data Scientist based in Curitiba, Brazil, working at the intersection of rigorous statistical thinking and real production systems. My work spans insurance underwriting, credit risk modeling, and clinical data — environments where model uncertainty isn't academic, it's a business risk.

My edge is combining Bayesian inference, survival analysis, and causal modeling with the engineering discipline to ship and maintain models in regulated environments.
